FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to buy a domain separately?

If you already own a domain, we'll connect it for you. If not, we can help you register one as part of your setup — either way, it's covered in your onboarding call.

Can you move my existing website to Haven Platform?

Yes. We'll review what you have, migrate your content, and check everything works before pointing your domain over — so there's no downtime for your customers.

What if my site needs something more custom later?

You can move up a tier at any time. We'll quote the extra work up front, and your existing site and content carry over.

How fast will my site actually be?

Every plan includes a content delivery network, so pages are served from a location close to each visitor rather than a single distant server.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes — if hosting with us isn't the right fit in your first 30 days, we'll refund what you've paid for hosting.

Ready for a website that just works?

Send a note with what you need, and we'll reply with a straightforward quote — usually within a day.
